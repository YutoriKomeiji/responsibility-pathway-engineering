# Recovery Pathway Reading

This note records a narrow reading of repair as recovery within Responsibility Pathway Engineering.

## Purpose

Recovery pathway reading describes how an interrupted, unclear, suspended, or damaged Responsibility Pathway can become actionable again.

It is a reading of repair, not a separate certification layer.

## Minimal reading

A recovery pathway exists when enough of the following have been restored for responsible action:

- context
- authority
- evidence
- return points
- review capacity
- next-state choice

The pathway may then be continued, stopped, corrected, compensated, redesigned, or closed by an authorized entity.

## Non-elimination boundary

Recovery does not mean that the original harm disappeared.

Recovery does not mean that loss, uncertainty, responsibility, or blame has been erased.

Recovery means that the pathway has been reconnected enough to be handled responsibly again.

## Relationship to repair

Use `docs/repair-model.md` for the main Repair Model.

Use `examples/repair-flow.yaml` for a minimal example that includes a recovery-pathway reading.

This note only clarifies the reading boundary.

## Boundary

This note is not a guarantee of justice, safety, legal compliance, fairness, full recovery, production readiness, certification, conformance, or AI final-responsibility transfer.
