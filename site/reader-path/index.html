Core responsibility path

Human request

A human or institution starts the work and keeps decision ownership reachable.

AI assists

AI may draft, summarize, recommend, or prepare evidence, but does not become the final responsible actor.

Evidence remains visible

Source references, assumptions, exclusions, and uncertainty should stay inspectable.

Human approval remains reachable

Approval, stop authority, and return points stay connected to human or institutional judgment.

Repair and reopening stay possible

If the path is incomplete, noisy, or wrong, the case can return to a repair owner.
