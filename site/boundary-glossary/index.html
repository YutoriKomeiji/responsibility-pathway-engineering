Boundary: This page explains project language. It is not legal advice, safety review, compliance review, fairness review, certification, production approval, or AI final-responsibility transfer.

Glossary of excluded readings

Certification

Excluded: A repository page, example, checker, or workflow does not certify an organization, product, or process.

Safe reading: It may provide a reviewable structure.

Validation

Excluded: Passing a bounded check is not end-to-end validation of a real-world decision.

Safe reading: It may confirm that selected fields or examples follow a local structure.

Production readiness

Excluded: A static page or sample file does not make RPE a production service, SDK, or runtime.

Safe reading: It is a reader path and construction surface.

Legal, safety, compliance, fairness

Excluded: These high-stakes reviews are not completed by the repository itself.

Safe reading: RPE can help route where such human review must remain visible.

Formal proof

Excluded: Lean or other formal material does not prove unstated real-world claims.

Safe reading: It can show stated properties under stated assumptions.

AI final responsibility

Excluded: AI assistance does not become the final responsible subject.

Safe reading: Human or institutional responsibility remains the return point.

Placement rule

  • Keep claims small and inspectable.
  • Keep non-claims close to the claims they limit.
  • Keep responsibility holders, stop authority, and repair owners visible.
  • Do not treat decoration, navigation, green checks, examples, or formalization as stronger proof than they are.

Reader note: This glossary helps avoid overreading. It does not reduce the need for real human review where law, safety, compliance, fairness, procurement, or production use is involved.

Human author remains responsible for definitions and claims. AI tools may assist drafting and implementation but do not become responsible authors.
